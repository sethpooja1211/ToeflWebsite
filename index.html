About TOEFL

TOEFL Infinite provides a 360-degree solution for TOEFL exam. Some of the popular features available on the app are SPEAKING, LISTENING, WRITING, READING, VOCABULARY.

SAMPLE RESPONSES Learn from sample audio responses of top users. EVALUATIONS FROM EXPERTS Get your responses evaluated by experts. The evaluation includes a full analysis of language use, delivery, content and topic development.
